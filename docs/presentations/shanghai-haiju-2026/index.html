The Problem

Precision medicine is built on biased foundations.

🌎
85% European bias
85% of genomic research is based on populations of European descent. 4.5 billion people are underrepresented in precision medicine.
📈
AI compounds inequity
LLMs trained on biased literature amplify existing gaps. Our research shows biobanks encode 2.7x isolation for Global South diseases.
🔒
Tools are inaccessible
Genomic analysis requires expensive cloud infrastructure. Labs in developing nations cannot access the tools they need most.
The populations with the greatest genetic diversity have the least genomic infrastructure.
The Solution

Three pillars for equitable genomics.

🦖
ClawBio
The execution layer. 46 agent-native bioinformatics skills. Runs on consumer laptops. No cloud needed. Every result is reproducible and auditable.
⚖️
HEIM
The measurement layer. Health Equity Informative Metrics. Quantifies research bias across 176 diseases, 70 biobanks, 13.1M publications. Under review at Nature Health.
📊
FairMed
The evidence layer. Empirically proves UK Biobank amplifies bias 2.7x for Global South diseases. First audit framework replicable for any biobank.
Together: measure the gap (HEIM), prove it is structural (FairMed), close it with tools (ClawBio).
